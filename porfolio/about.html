About Me

Hi, I am Zaid
and I am a 20-year-old pursuing BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) from Silver Oak University. As I enter my final year, I have developed a solid foundation in various aspects of programming and technology. I have acquired basic knowledge in web development, web designing, Java development, and game development, which has sparked my interest in the field.

Programming and problem-solving have become my passion, and I thoroughly enjoy the challenge of tackling complex coding tasks. I believe that the ability to analyze problems and find efficient solutions through coding is an essential skill in today's technology-driven world.

Aside from my technical pursuits, I am also an avid drawer. I find immense joy and creative expression in sketching and drawing various subjects. Art allows me to think outside the box and explore different perspectives, which often complements my problem-solving abilities in programming.

Throughout my academic journey, I have consistently demonstrated dedication, perseverance, and a thirst for knowledge. I am eager to apply my skills and continue learning in a professional setting. I am open to new opportunities and challenges that will allow me to grow both as a programmer and an artist.
